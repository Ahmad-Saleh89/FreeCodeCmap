About Ahmad Saleh

I spent one semester at that level and then I was eligible to enter any program I wanted as an international student. Unfortunately, UNL did not transfer most of the classes I took at the Pharmacy School of Damascus University and I was left with no choice other than taking numerous numbers of classes in order to acquire a degree from UNL.
